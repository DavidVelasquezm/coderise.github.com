How it works

Length and Structure

Coderise’s pilot program, offered absolutely free, will begin October 9th, 2012. The first class will be taught by two instructors and will consist of 20 students in the city of Medellín, Colombia. Over the course of 8 weeks the students will learn the principles of Computer Programming and Software Development with a heavy emphasis on creating web applications. To further develop the students’ learning they will have the opportunity to engage with tech entrepreneurs and engineers from Colombia and from the U.S.

During the first half of each class, students will learn the concepts necessary to build their very own web application. The second half of each class will provide students an opportunity to develop a web application of their own throughout the program, simulating real world context. At the end of the program the class will demo their finalized projects to their peers and instructors.

Learning Objectives

At the end of the program students should be able to:

  • Identify and understand web technologies such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, and databases
  • Explain and teach other students what they have learned
  • Know how to learn
  • Understand that failure is part of the learning process
  • Understand the benefits of Open Source software and how to contribute to it
  • Identify problems in their communities and propose software-aided solutions
  • Create a Minimal Viable Product (MVP)

Coderise Activities and Events

  • Recreational visits to Parque Arví with the team
  • Meetups with the tech startup and software programming community in Medellín
  • Mini Coderise Hackathon (not yet confirmed)

Mentors of Coderise

Each Saturday, after regular class from 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM, there will be informal talks with various Coderise mentors, successful professionals from the tech world. These will share their experiences, their trajectory throughout various initiatives, and will be able to talk one-on-one with Coderise students. [Read a bit more on mentors here.]
