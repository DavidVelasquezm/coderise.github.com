ABOUT CODERISE

Launched in Spring 2012, Coderise is an international nonprofit organization working to empower kids from developing nations by teaching them how to build great web applications. With support from multinational strongholds including Socialatom, Intergrupo, and Github, Coderise programs work to inspire and equip high school kids with the skills and resources to jumpstart their interest in the technology and programming sectors.
Together with leading educators, engineers, and entrepreneurs, Coderise has developed a new model for engaging students with the programming basics necessary to build globally competitive software applications, pairing intensive instruction in Javascript, HTML5 and CSS with high-touch mentorship led by the industry's top developers and entrepreneurs. Coderise empowered high school students from 11 different schools and 5 metropolitan districts of Medellín, Colombia in its inaugural program and will launch in cities across the country in 2014.
We are a small, dynamic group of individuals passionate about changing the world. We thrive on working in a collaborative, fast-paced environment. We represent a diversity of backgrounds, a wealth of experience, and a ton of fun. Our team is spread throughout Colombia and New York.

POSITION

Coderise is searching for a strategic, results-oriented Managing Director to lead its program expansion to markets across Latin America in 2014. S/he will be responsible for ensuring operational efficiency and programmatic success as the organization enters a period of fast-paced growth. S/he is tasked with the streamlining the delivery of Coderise programs as well as business development for the organization. The ideal candidate has superior business relations skills and is an excellent manager with proven experience scaling programming and operations in a metrics-driven environment.
In this newly established role, the Managing Director will report to the Board of Advisors and manage a growing team of program staff. The successful candidate has 5-10 years of experience, with at least two years in a senior management role.

Working closely with the Executive Director, the Managing Director is responsible for the timely and successful execution of the following:

  • Develop performance metrics to ensure consistent, high-quality work product of program staff; manage evaluation and goal setting for all employees and programs
Program Operational Management:
  • Work with the Board of Directors to fundraise and make the organization financially sustainable
  • Develop, direct, and deliver successful Coderise programs, launching 2-4 programs in 2014

  • Develop framework for program evaluation, ensuring programmatic success, long-term effectiveness and organizational efficiency at scale
  • Develop and refine program models to meet short- and long-term goals for scale and efficacy
Leadership:
  • Manage relationships with key stakeholders, funders and partners to provide sufficient resources, space and services to support program delivery
  • Cultivate relationships to ensure partner satisfaction and continued investments in Coderise programs
