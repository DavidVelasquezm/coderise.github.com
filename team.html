Our Team

Andrea Cornejo / Program Director

Andrea is a serial social entrepreneur, and an economic development and impact evaluation specialist originally form Peru. Andrea has founded several initiatives, including EmprendeSocial, the first online publication for social entrepreneurship in Latin America, Proyecto Viña Vieja, a community development initiative in Peru. Andrea currently is director of Coderise, an NGO focused on empowering high school students of developing nations by teaching them how to creat great web applications and by connecting them to the dynamic tech start-up and software programmer community to draw inspiration.

andrea@coderise.com

Camilo Aguilar - Curriculum Advisor / Asesor Técnico de Curriculo

Throughout his years of working, he has contributed extensively to open-source projects of his own, especially in the NodeJS community. Camilo has worked for recognized startups in New York City such as BugLabs and currently works for Onswipe, while also being one of the principal leaders of Coderise.
