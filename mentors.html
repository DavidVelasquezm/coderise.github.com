Coderise Mentors

Each Saturday, after regular class from 8:00 AM to 11:00 AM, there will be informal talks with various Coderise mentors, successful professionals from the tech world.

- Andrés Barreto (@andresbarreto)

Colombian entrepreneur, co-founder of Onswipe, a platform for tablet publishing and advertising. He previously cofounded Grooveshark, a leading music streaming app, Socialatom Group, a Tech and PR agency that also promotes entrepreneurship in the region with startup and tech blog PulsoSocial.


- Alex Torrenegra (@torrenegra)

Founder of Torrenegra Labs, tech startup incubator in Bogotá, Colombia, and founder of VoiceBunny, BookingMarkets, WeHostels, HubBOG, and others.


- Daniel Loreto (@DanielLoreto)

Tech entrepreneur and software programmer. After working for Google, founded social data analytic startup Julpan, and currently is on the engineering team for Twitter.


- Gisella Borja (@gigiborja)

CTO of Booking Markets, online platform focused on travel and stay.


- Juan Pablo Buritica (@buritica)

Colombian entrepreneur and software engineer, he is currently the VP of Engineering at Onswipe. He is also founder and director of Bogotaconf, a conference dedicated to the highest quality software engineering topics. Formerly, he cofounded and was the Lead Engineer at Panelfly, a mobile comics distribution app.
